Air Fried Salmon

Description

Air Fried Salmon is a common dish that i eat to make sure that i get protein, carbs, and vegetables. I usually would make broccoli and white rice along with my salmon. It is a very good dish and dosent require a lot of ingredients nor time.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Seasonings of Choice
  • 1(6 ounce) fillet salmon fillet, thawed if frozen and patted dry
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 cup white rice

Steps

  1. Clean Salmon and season well with seasonings of choice.
  2. After you seasoned the salmon fillet, place it into the air fryer basket. Air-fry at 375 degrees F for 8-12 minutes.
  3. Open the basket half way through and place the Broccoli around the fillet as best you can, being careful not to cover it.
  4. Air-fry at 375 degrees F until salmon is cooked through and Broccoli is tender, 6 to 7 more minutes.
  5. Cook Rice in Rice Cooker or Use instructions on the bag/box
